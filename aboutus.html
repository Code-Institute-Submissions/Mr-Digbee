About Us

Fall In Love With Your Garden & Home Again

All You Need To Know About Us

Mr Digbee is owned and run by Garry Gulliver. Garry has over 20 years’ experience in creating beautiful gardens and maintaining both residential and commercial properties to the highest standards. Garry is fully trained; friendly and professional and will ensure that you experience a seamless journey whether he is creating a brand new garden for you or maintaining an existing one. Garry’s extensive knowledge ensures that we will never be short of ideas if you are struggling for inspiration and we will gladly work with you to achieve a result that perfectly meets your needs and budget. Our prices are always competitive and we give all of our customer’s exceptional value for money. Every project is different which is why we offer a FREE, no obligation quotation. We are a company that values, above all, its reputation, in order to maintain this we strive to give each and every one of our customers a level of service and expertise that you will want to tell your friends and family about. Mr Digbee is based in Salisbury, Wiltshire. Being a mobile service we cover the entire County of Wiltshire and will often work in neighbouring Counties such as Hampshire, Dorset, Somerset and Berkshire.
